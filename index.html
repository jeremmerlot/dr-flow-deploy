Initiative 2Increase "While using app" share
Better permission prompt timing & copy reduces the 33% who deny location — growing the addressable audience for Auto‑FMT and SignalBox checks.
Initiative 3Let guests see Delay Repay components
Guest trips are currently a dead end — no location data, no resolution. This routes every guest trip through the same location‑based flow as logged‑in users, closing that gap entirely (full coverage, already in build). It also closes the guest gap on Advance tickets — those need no confirmation logic once a guest is recognised, so their industry‑wide coverage moves from 80% to 100%.
